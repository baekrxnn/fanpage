EXO is a 9 member boy group from South Korea. Out of the nine members, eight are Korean and one member is Chinese. EXO debuted on April 8, 2012 in Seoul, South Korea. EVer since debut, they have released 4 full-length albums, along with repackage albums, as well as 4 winter special albums and 2 mini albums. EXO releases albums in Korean, Chinese, and Japanese, and have been slaying the charts all over the place. They are the first group to win "Album of the Year" for 5 consecutive years at Mnet Asian Music Awards.
